About Me

My name is Philip Sheppard. I practice private counselling in Chandlers Ford, near the junction of the M3 and M27, Hampshire. I have 20 years’ experience working with men and women between the ages of 18 and 80, including people from lesbian, gay and other minority groups, and with people of different religious beliefs and none. I offer individual and couple counselling. I also provide supervision and mentoring to other counsellors and counselling students.

I seek to offer a respectful environment in which clients feel safe to explore their experiences and explore new ways forward. As a Member of BACP I am bound by its Ethical Framework for Good Practice in Counselling and Psychotherapy and am subject to their Professional Conduct Procedure. My work is fully insured.
