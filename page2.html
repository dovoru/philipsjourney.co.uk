About Counselling

We all experience ups and downs in life. There are good times and bad times, difficult times and easy times, painful times and joyful times, setbacks and breakthroughs. This is normal. No one has it easy all the time.

When we can face our problems in life and overcome them we grow our ability to experience life and we move on. Life is an enjoyable challenge. But sometimes it is not so easy. Sometimes a person who is normally on top of life finds themselves confronted by a shock, a sudden and unexpected loss or change of circumstances for which they were not prepared. For other people the problem may be more long standing. They may find that life is on top of them and getting them down most of the time, that life seems to be one long struggle. They may be inclined to depression. They may cope for many years putting up with an unsatisfying life, but reach a point where they cannot stand it any longer. They want to change but cannot see a way forward. They feel stuck or trapped, unable to escape or move on.

At times of difficulty sharing our experience with a skilled listener who is outside the situation may make a big difference. A skilled listener can help us to make sense of what is happening, work through any emotions, find the resources to recover and move on. We may discover that what appeared to be an insoluble problem has a solution. Pain that was unbearable may become bearable. We may find a way out of the trap. We may find that what appeared to be a setback is a breakthrough to a new beginning.

A skilled counsellor is trained to pay attention to you and listen in this creative way. Counselling may make all the difference between staying stuck and moving on with your life.
