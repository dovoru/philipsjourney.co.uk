Hil's Memories

These are just some of my memories, and this is not yet complete. I just knocked this up quickly in 10 minutes so that my Dad could see that I have access to his site.This page is very much in draft form, and is not intented to be part of the final website. It is for example purposes only.....

 
  • Always feeling very safe and protected while Dad was around
  • Dad removing all of the wolves and monsters from my room
  • Waiting for headlights to turn into the drive before I could sleep if Dad was out
  • Walking up the road to meet Dad from work and get my side of the story across before Mum could
  • Asking Dad for ice creams on holidays abroad while Mum was out of sight
  • Dad running out of a shop in Germany and getting into the car just in time to stop it rolling into traffic after we accidentally released the handbrake
  • Dad rushing into the tent and putting out the shirt that I had set fire to with the lamp
  • Finding a caravan so that we were safe when a big wind threatened our tent in France
  • Coming round to a house where I was babysitting, and resurrecting the tv after I had spilt water down the back
  • Helping me with my calculus excercises - about 20 years after doing any calculus!
